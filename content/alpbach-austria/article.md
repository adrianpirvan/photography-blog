# Alpbach - Austria, | Between Two Rains

**Category:** Travel
**Date:** Apr '25

---

Brought the car from Romania to the Netherlands, and decided to sneak in a short vacation along the way. Somewhere between "this trip is practical" and "why is the weather like this," we stopped in Alpbach for a couple of days.

It rained the whole time, because of course it did. But from time to time the fog was showing up turning the place into a moody postcard factory. Wooden cabins, winding roads, mist everywhere.

Not bad for a detour.
