# Snowbound in Amsterdamse Bos, | an Unlikely Winter Surprise

**Category:** Life
**Date:** 05/01/26

---

After a rare and weirdly disruptive 10cm snowfall brought the entire Netherlands to a halt (because apparently we live on a delicate infrastructure made of wishful thinking), Amsterdamse Bos became strangely unreal. 

By afternoon, the storm gave up, the sun cut through, and a soft fog rolled in as steam rose off the snow revealing a surreal golden-pink haze that made my camera cry little pixel tears of joy. I've never seen anything like it before.
